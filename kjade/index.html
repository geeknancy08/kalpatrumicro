Kalpataru Jade Residences

Kalpataru Jade Residences is an unusual blend of both modern and sophisticated architecture developed by Kalpataru Developers Ltd. Positioned in a well developed locality of Baner, Pune , kalpataru Jade floor plan offers 3 BHK and 4BHK units spanning over 1138 sqft to 3380 sqft. Overview

Kalpataru Jade Residences at Baner in Pune is spread over 9 acres, with only 18% of building footprint and sprawling 3 acres open area + vehicle free zone. The project comprises eight towers with luxurious 3BHK and 4BHK apartments nestled in lush greens, set against the backdrop of the picturesque Baner Hills.
